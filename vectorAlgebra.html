Vector Algebra

Vector are objects that have both magnitude and direction. Understanding vector is important because they model motion in the real world. Vectors can be added, substracted, and have multiple other equations and manipulations done to them. This calculator will allow you to perform addition and subtration of 2D vectors, along with finding their dot product, which can in turn be used to find the angle between them. Angles in the calculated are measured in degrees.
