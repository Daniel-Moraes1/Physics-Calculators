One Dimension Kinematics

The purpose of this calculator is to solve problems related to one directional, forceless motion. While this may initially appear unhelpful in real world applications, one dimension kinematics can be use to solve problems such as measuring a sprinters acceleration or race time, or a car's velocity.

It is important to understand the relationship between Position, Velocity, and Acceleration. Velocity * Time = Change in Position, and Acceleration * Time = Change in Velocity. In other words, Velocity is the derivative of Position with respect to Time, and Acceleration is the derivative of Velocity with respect to Time.

*Note: Leave values blank if unknown, not 0. By the nature of these equations, some calculations may occur if impossible values are input*
