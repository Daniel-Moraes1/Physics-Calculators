Collisions

There are three types of collisions: Perfectly Elastic, Inelastic, and Perfectly Inelastic.

Perfectly Elastic Colisions

Both Momentum and Energy are conserved in a Elastic Collision.

Inelastic Collisions

Momentum is conserved, Energy is lost, and the objects remain separate in an Inelastic Collision.

Perfectly Inelastic Collisions

Momentum is conserved, Energy is lost, and the objects combine to form one object in a Perfectly Inelastic Collision.
